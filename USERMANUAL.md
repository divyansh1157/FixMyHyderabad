# User Manual — FixMyHyderabad

> A step-by-step guide for Hyderabad citizens on how to use the FixMyHyderabad Civic Pulse platform.

---

## What is FixMyHyderabad?

FixMyHyderabad is a community-driven platform where Hyderabad citizens can:
- **Report** civic issues like potholes, waterlogging, garbage, and broken streetlights
- **Confirm** issues reported by others to increase their urgency
- **Track** the live status of issues across different areas of the city

---

## Getting Started

No account or login needed. Just open the app and start reporting.

Open the app at: `https://fix-my-hyderabad.vercel.app`

---

## How to Report an Issue

1. Click the **"Report an Issue"** button on the home screen
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Category** — select one: Pothole, Garbage, Waterlogging, Streetlight, or Other
   - **Title** — short description e.g. "Large pothole near KPHB signal"
   - **Description** — more detail about the issue (optional but helpful)
   - **Photo** — attach a photo from your phone or camera (optional)
3. Click **"Use My Location"** — this auto-fills your GPS coordinates
4. Click **"Submit Report"**

Your issue will appear on the live feed immediately.

> **Tip:** The more detail and a clear photo you provide, the more seriously the issue gets taken by GHMC authorities.

---

## How to Confirm an Issue

If you've seen an issue someone else reported:

1. Find the issue in the live feed
2. Click the **"I Confirm This Issue"** button on the report card
3. The confirmation count goes up by 1

Each person can confirm an issue only once. The button will be disabled after you confirm.

> **Why confirm?** Higher confirmation counts push issues to the top of the urgency list, making it more likely GHMC acts on them faster.

## Understanding Issue Status

Each report card shows a status badge:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟠 Active | Issue reported, not yet addressed |
| 🔵 In Review | GHMC has acknowledged the issue |
| 🟢 Resolved | Issue has been fixed |

## Tips for Good Reports

- **Be specific in the title** — "Pothole on KPHB Road 5 near bus stop" is better than "Pothole"
- **Use your live location** — GPS-tagged reports are more actionable
- **Add a photo** — visual proof increases credibility
- **Confirm issues you've seen** — even if you didn't report it, confirming helps

---

## Privacy

- No account or personal data is required
- Your location is only used to tag the report — it is not stored or shared beyond the report itself
- Confirmations are tracked anonymously using a browser session ID

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I report an issue without sharing my location?**
You can type in the area name manually, but GPS tagging makes your report more precise and useful.

**What happens after I report an issue?**
Your report goes live on the feed immediately. As more citizens confirm it, it rises in urgency. This data is visible to GHMC authorities for prioritization.

**Can I edit or delete my report after submitting?**
Not in the current version. Make sure the details are correct before submitting.

**Why can I only confirm once?**
To keep the data credible. One confirmation per person ensures the count reflects genuine community agreement, not spam.

**The location button isn't working — what do I do?**
Make sure you've allowed location access in your browser. In Chrome, click the lock icon in the address bar → allow Location.
